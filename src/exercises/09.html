Hosted WordPress with Custom Theme

For this exercise, you will deploy a WordPress site to your hosting (It needn't be the same site as the previous exercise). Your deployed WordPress site will utilize a custom theme designed and developed by you. Your theme needn't be completely polished, but should demonstrate your ability to add custom styling to a WordPress site.

We will allocate time in class to ensure you are able to deploy a WordPress site, but you may need time out of class to complete this exercise.
